About PDFTools Pro

Your browser-based PDF powerhouse

Our Mission

PDFTools Pro was built with a simple belief: PDF tools should be free, private, and accessible to everyone. No uploads, no servers, no data collection — just powerful PDF processing that runs entirely in your browser.

Why We Built This

Most online PDF tools require you to upload your documents to a remote server. This means your sensitive data — contracts, personal documents, financial records — leaves your control. We wanted a better way.

By leveraging modern web technologies like WebAssembly and client-side JavaScript libraries (pdf-lib, PDF.js, Tesseract.js), every operation runs locally on your machine. Your files never touch a network.

What We Offer

16 tools covering the most common PDF workflows:

  • Merge, split, compress, and rotate PDFs
  • Convert between PDFs and images
  • Add passwords, watermarks, and page numbers
  • Edit PDFs with annotations and drawings
  • Extract text with OCR in 12+ languages
  • Convert PDFs to Word documents
  • Extract table data to CSV/Excel
  • Build interactive PDF forms
  • Sign PDFs with drawn, typed, or uploaded signatures
  • Batch process multiple PDFs at once

Our Principles

  • Privacy first — Zero server uploads. Everything stays in your browser.
  • Always free — No paid tiers, no hidden limits, no watermarks.
  • Works offline — Once loaded, no internet connection is needed.
  • No account required — Just open and use. No sign-ups, no tracking.
  • Cross-platform — Desktop, tablet, or mobile. Any modern browser.

The Technology

PDFTools Pro is powered by:

  • PDF-lib — PDF creation and modification
  • PDF.js — PDF rendering from Mozilla
  • Tesseract.js — OCR engine in JavaScript

All three are open-source libraries that run entirely client-side.
