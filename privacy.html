Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

1. Introduction

PDFTools Pro ("we", "our", "the Service") operates the website at pdf-tools-pro.vercel.app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. No Data Collection

PDFTools Pro processes all files entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. We do not upload, store, transmit, or have access to any files you process. Your PDFs, images, and any other files never leave your device.

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Your PDF files or their contents
  • Images or documents you upload
  • Extracted text or data
  • Personal identification information

3. Third-Party Services

Our Service loads the following third-party libraries from CDNs to enable PDF processing:

  • pdf-lib — PDF creation and manipulation
  • PDF.js — PDF rendering and text extraction
  • Tesseract.js — Optical Character Recognition (OCR)

These libraries are loaded from unpkg.com CDN. Your browser may cache these files locally. No personal data is sent to these providers during normal use.

4. Google AdSense

Our Service uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may read more about Google's privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

Our Service does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense for ad personalization and frequency capping. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

Since all processing occurs client-side, we retain no data. Any file you upload is processed within your browser session and removed when you close the page or clear your browser's memory.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
